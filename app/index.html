    ParcelHero® provides £50 free loss/damage cover on every shipment*. To add additional cover, enter the value of your goods and click the plus sign.

  • Don't deliver without a signature £1.00

    ParcelHero® requires a signature for each delivery, however some countries allow the courier to ‘driver release' the package (this means they are allowed to deliver to a pre-determined location at the receiver's location such as a porch or with a neighbour) without obtaining a signature from the receiver. By selecting the ‘signature required' option, you can prevent the courier from releasing the parcel without signature.

  • Deliver to a home address +10%

    Some carriers charge more to deliver to a home address, if you're sending a package to a residential address, click this option to include the surcharge in your quote. Please note if you chose to collect your parcel from an access point, the home delivery surcharge does not apply.

  • Guaranteed delivery £2.20

    All our timed delivery services come with a money-back guarantee. For all other services we offer the option to upgrade to a money-back guarantee.

    Please note:

    1. The money-back guarantee applies when we fail to deliver by the commitment date due to a carrier error.
    2. It does not apply where delivery is delayed due to circumstances outside of our control such as bad weather or a customs clearance delay.
